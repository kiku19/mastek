ABOUT   MASTEK

MASTEK is an ISO 9001:2015 certified and evolving transnational company having a noteworthy presence in the Oil & Gas industry. We extend our services towards Civil, Mechanical, Electrical and Instrumentation works with Engineering, Procurement and Construction as a complete package. Ever since the company's inception in 2003, it has shown proficient progress and has earned the valuable trust of its customers. The company is supporting the Indian Oil Industry and contributing towards the development of the nation in every possible manner. MASTEK has been consistently showing its capabilities and commitments towards the Oil & Gas Industry and has been proving its worth by completing projects in time upholding the Health, Safety, Environment and Quality initiatives.

 OUR CAPABILITES

• Engineering Services

• Manpower Supply

• Project Management

• Cost Estimation and Pre-bid assistance for Tendering

• Construction & Procurement

QUALITY MANAGEMENT

Quality Management is vital to the services provided by MASTEK. Our clients determine what quality is. Our aim is to deliver more than the client expects of us. We want to achieve this by deploying personal know-how, experience and the specific skills of each employee. The Company acquires ISO 9001:2008 from UKAS in 2013.

 OUR MANAGEMENT

Mr M.Suresh Kumar Managing partner of the company, Completed a Bachelor Degree in Mechanical Engineering in SRM University, TN, India
He had started his career as junior engineer in M/S Aban Constructions and then he worked in L&T & ECC. He started his own construction company in the name Mastek Engineering in 2007 with a vision to provide services to the Oil & Gas sector in India.
Currently we had completed several projects all over India and also we set a bench mark in Oil & Gas Industry.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to achieve consistent identity in the deliverance of highly qualified services and adding values to the clients through pro-activity, transparency in communication and on time deliverance of projects through excellent performance. We practice sustainability to safeguard our environment and maintain the highest levels of professionalism, honesty and trust in our relationship with clients & employees and affiliates.

OUR VISION

Be the india's premier engineering, construction, and project management organization by achieving extraordinary results for our customers, building satisfying careers for our people, and earning a fair return on the value we deliver.
